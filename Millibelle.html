MILLIBELLE THE THIEF BANKER

Originally met in Fog Canyon, near the Queen's Station, she offers the player a totally legitimate banking service, and you can open one for the low, low price of 100 Geo, to store your Geo in a safe area and safe hands.

Once the player gives her at least 2550 Geo and rests at a bench or uses a stag station, and come back to her, they realize she is now gone, along with the given Geo

You can find her in the Pleasure House in the City of Tears, chilling in a Spring, beat her up, smack her shell around, and she'll drop your precious Geo, and give you more, 50% more, who the hell else she took the extra Geo from is none of our business, just take it too, they probably don't need it, right?

Tips

Don't open her bank unless you need more money, but you already have Lemm, you oaf.

If you open her bank, make sure you already have a simple key to open the Pleasure house, or unlock the Pleasure House beforehand, give her all your Geo, then beat the life out of her shell, sadly, you cannot kill her.
